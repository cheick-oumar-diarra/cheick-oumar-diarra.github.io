Computational physicist with a PhD in Physics and expertise in first-principles modelling of exciton dynamics,
heat transport, and molecular processes in materials and biomolecular systems. Skilled in density functional theory,
ab initio molecular dynamics, high-performance computing, scientific programming, and data analysis.
Research interests include organic semiconductors, energy materials, biomolecular modelling,
and the development of computational approaches connecting atomistic simulations with experimentally relevant properties.
Experienced in university teaching and open to research, teaching, and interdisciplinary collaborations in academic or industrial environments.
